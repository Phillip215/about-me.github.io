    • Why Software Engineering

    My path to coding in general was paved with me working jobs at maximum effort and feeling like it was leading to minimal gain overall. When the pandemic happened my job was considered essential so I thought I would be secure in my position. Unfortunately the place I had worked was losing business do to the pandemic and I was one of the people they let go. For about two months I stayed inside waiting for the nightmare to be over so I could begin my job search. That's when it hit me that I would have to start from square one at a business for the third time in my life. That is when I decided to gain skills in Software Engineering not only for the new career path but also to put my in position for goals I have outside of the workplace.
